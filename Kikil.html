KIKIL

Ingredients

    • ¼ pot lamb meat, without the bones being removed
    • ¼ pot potatoes, largely chopped
    • 1 pot full of cold water
    • 3 cups onions, sliced
    • ½ cup green chili pepper, chopped
    • ½ cup cloves of garlic
    • 2 tbsp turmeric
